About Us

Who We Are

Fitout Bureau Interiors is one of the leading interior design and fitout company in Dubai. With a passion for creating stunning spaces, we specialize in providing comprehensive architectural and interior design solutions, fitout services, joinery, MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing), authority approvals, and custom-made bespoke furniture production. Since our establishment in 2013, we have consistently delivered exceptional results that exceed our clients' expectations. We understand that every space has its unique identity and purpose. With our expertise in materials and finishes, concept design, detailed architectural and procurement, FF and E (Furniture, Fixtures and Equipment) specification and procurement,interior design and fitout solutions, we bring your vision to life. Whether it's an office, retail store, hospitality establishment, or residential property, our team of dedicated professionals ensures that each project is executed with the highest level of craftsmanship and attention to detail.

Led by our esteemed CEO, Mr. Abdul Jaleel, Fitout Bureau Interiors has earned a reputation for being one of the top interior fit-out companies in Dubai and the UAE. With his visionary leadership and industry expertise, we have successfully completed numerous prestigious projects across various sectors. Our commitment to excellence, innovation, and client satisfaction sets us apart as the preferred choice for fit out services in the region.

SERVICES

What We Offer

Interior Fit Out

We specialize in delivering turnkey fit out solutions. Whether it's an office, retail store, or hospitality establishment, our experienced team ensures that your space is not only aesthetically pleasing but also functional and efficient.

Design and Build

With our design and build service, creativity and construction come together in a streamlined process. From initial concepts to final touches, we oversee every stage to ensure a unified vision, delivering projects that are both efficient and high in quality.

Mep

We integrate Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing systems seamlessly into your space, prioritising both functionality and safety. Whether it’s advanced HVAC solutions or precise electrical and plumbing work, we ensure everything operates smoothly and meets the highest standards.

Joinery

Our custom joinery solutions bring craftsmanship to life with tailored woodwork that enhances any environment. Whether it's bespoke cabinetry or unique furniture, each piece is meticulously crafted to offer both elegance and practicality.

Authority Approvals

We handle all aspects of authority approvals, ensuring that your project complies with local regulations and standards. From documentation to liaising with authorities, we streamline the process to keep your project on track.
